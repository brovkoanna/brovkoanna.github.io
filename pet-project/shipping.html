Shipping and payment

Delivery methods

  1. Delivery by courier in the city of Dnipro

    Free delivery by courier in the city is also possible when placing an order for more than 1000 UAH.

  2. Delivery by "Nova Poshta"

    We deliver by Nova Poshta to any region of Ukraine. Delivery in Ukraine is carried out by the Nova Poshta service. You can pick up the parcel at a Nova Poshta branch or arrange delivery to the address.

Payment methods

  1. Cash payment upon receipt of the order

    If you do not want to prepay for the order, you have the opportunity to pay the courier in cash at the time of receipt of the parcel. To make the process as comfortable as possible for both you and the courier, we recommend that you prepare the required amount in advance.

  2. Cash payment upon receipt at post offices and parcel delivery points (cash on delivery)

    When you receive a parcel at a Nova Poshta post office, you can pay in cash or by card. Thus, you pay only the amount of the order without hidden fees, commissions and services of transport companies.

  3. Cashless payment when placing an order

    Holders of Visa and MasterCard cards can pay for the order in full at the time of ordering. This payment option will save time when receiving the parcel. When you meet with the courier, managers of the pickup point or post office, you will just need to pick up the order, having previously provided a document confirming the identity of the recipient.
